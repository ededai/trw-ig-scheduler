## The big idea

Stop letting the feed feel like one big template loop. Each post should stand alone — different topic, different visual rhythm, different emotional register. Same 6 series, but the *vibe* of each post is uniquely its own. A scroll through the feed should feel like flipping through a magazine, not refreshing a status page.

## Visual rules (locked across all 21 posts)

- 1080×1350 portrait, never 1080×1080
- TRW orange + canonical palette only (per `reference_trw_logo.md`)
- Approved IG post fonts (per `reference_ig_post_font_template.md`)
- No em-dashes, no en-dashes (per `feedback_no_em_dashes.md`)
- $ not S$ (per `feedback_trw_ig_price_format.md`)
- Image fills full frame — zero bottom padding (per `feedback_trw_ig_no_dead_space.md`)
- Real Singapore feel where photo-led — no glossy fake-workshop stock (per `feedback_trw_no_fake_workshop_shots.md`)
- All workshop photos auto-sanitised for carplates — TRW logo overlay, never black box (per `feedback_carplate_logo_masking.md`)

## Anti-AI-slop check (mandatory per `feedback_anti_ai_slop_skills.md`)

- Banned: "delve", "tapestry", "in conclusion", "boasts", "pivotal", "navigate", "embark"
- Sentences run short. Singapore-mechanic voice — direct, dry, occasionally wry.
- No motivational copy. No corporate cadence. No "we believe…" openers.
