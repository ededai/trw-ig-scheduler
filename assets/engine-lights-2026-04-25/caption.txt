3 lights that say your engine is unhappy.

Every driver sees them. Most guess. A few panic.
Here's what each one actually means — and which ones mean pull over now.

⚠️ CHECK ENGINE (amber)
Your car's computer found a fault. Could be a loose fuel cap. Could be an oxygen sensor. Could be a failing catalytic converter. The light alone won't tell you which. A scan will.

🔴 OIL PRESSURE (red)
Your engine has lost oil pressure. Without pressure, moving parts grind against each other. Damage starts in seconds. Pull over safely, switch off, call us.

🔴 ENGINE TEMPERATURE (red)
Your engine is overheating. Coolant is low, the pump has failed, or the thermostat is stuck. Even a minute of overheating can warp the cylinder head. Pull over. Switch off. Let it cool.
