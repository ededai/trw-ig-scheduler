2013-2018 Toyota Altis. The reliable sedan.

The 11th-gen Altis (E170) is one of the easiest cars to live with in SG. But "easiest" doesn't mean "no maintenance." If you own one, watch these four:

🟠 CVT shudder. Vibration around 40-60 km/h is almost always the CVT fluid asking for a change. Don't wait for the 100k brochure interval - SG stop-start traffic ages fluid faster.

🟠 Front wheel bearings. Past 120,000 km, a low hum that rises with speed = bearings on the way out. Common in SG-driven Altis.

🟠 Cooling coil (A/C evaporator). SG humidity breeds mould inside. Musty cabin air or weak cold air = coil failing.

🟠 Engine oil intervals. Don't stretch beyond 10,000 km even if Toyota's manual allows more. SG heat + traffic shorten oil life.
