2016-2021 Honda Civic FC. The everyday icon.

SG's perennial sedan favourite. Two engines, four predictable wear stories. Three hit every Civic, one is turbo-only.

🔴 Oil dilution (1.5T only). The L15B7 turbo mixes fuel into the oil on short SG trips. Oil level rises, smells like petrol on the dipstick. Honda issued a TSB. Check level monthly, change earlier than the manual says.

🔴 CVT shudder on take-off. Both 1.5T and 1.6 NA. Vibration when pulling away from rest = tired CVT fluid. Change at 60-80,000 km, not the lifetime brochure interval. SG stop-start traffic ages it faster.

🔴 A/C compressor failure. The 10th-gen Civic famously chews compressors. SG humidity and constant aircon use shorten life further. Listen for clutch-cycling or weak cold air.

🔴 Lower control arm bushings. Audible clunk over speedhumps and uneven roads at 80-100,000 km. Not dangerous immediately, but ride feel goes off. Cheap part, transformative refresh.
