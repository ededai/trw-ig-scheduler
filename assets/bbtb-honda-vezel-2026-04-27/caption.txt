2014-2021 Honda Vezel / HR-V. The everywhere SUV.

You can't drive 5 minutes in SG without seeing one. And for good reason - the RU-gen Vezel is roomy, efficient, and easy on the wallet. But every owner should know these four:

🔴 DCT gearbox (hybrid only). Pre-2016 hybrids were recalled for low-speed jerking. Later units need careful fluid + software service. If yours is jerky in traffic, get it scanned.

🔴 Low-pressure fuel pump. Honda's own service campaign covered this. Stalling or won't-start on a petrol Vezel? Check this first.

🔴 Cooling coil. SG humidity is brutal on A/C evaporators. Weak cold air or intermittent cooling = coil failing.

🔴 VTC actuator rattle. Cold-start rattle from the engine bay = clogged VTC actuator. Classic Earth Dreams issue. Cheap to fix early, expensive if ignored.
