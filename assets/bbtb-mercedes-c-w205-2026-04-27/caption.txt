Mercedes-Benz C-Class W205 · 2014-2021. The German exec sedan.

SG's go-to luxury sedan. Refined, comfortable, and (when looked after) genuinely durable. But the W205 has its own service rhythm - these four come up most often at the workshop:

🔵 Timing chain rattle (M274 engines). A few seconds of rattle on cold start = stretched chain or weak tensioner. Don't ignore. Fix early, save the engine.

🔵 Transmission fluid. Mercedes calls 7G / 9G-Tronic fluid "lifetime." It isn't. Service at 80,000 km regardless. Saves the gearbox.

🔵 Electronic parking brake. The motor-on-caliper seizes if the car sits often. Climbs to a four-figure repair. Use it weekly to keep it free.

🔵 A/C blower motor resistor. SG humidity kills the resistor. Fan stops working on lower speeds first. Cheap if caught early.
