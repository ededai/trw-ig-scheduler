2018-2023 Toyota Camry. The corporate cruiser.

The XV70 Camry is SG's quiet executive choice. Hybrid option, comfortable ride, Toyota reliability. But "Toyota reliable" still means service rhythm. Four things owners should track:

⚪ 2.5L A25A oil consumption. Some petrol units sip oil between services. Dipstick monthly - especially before long drives.

⚪ Hybrid battery cooling fan. Dust gathers behind the rear seat fan. Block the fan, fry the battery. Clean it every service.

⚪ 12V auxiliary battery. The hybrid's small AGM dies at 5-7 years. Random electrical glitches start there.

⚪ Engine motor mounts. Vibration through the cabin at idle = mounts going. Cheap to swap, transformative for ride feel.
