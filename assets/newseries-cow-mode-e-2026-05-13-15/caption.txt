Forget these numbers. Big problem.

Engine oil: 7,500km or 6 months. Brake fluid: 2 years or 40,000km. Timing belt: 100,000km or 7 years.

Manufacturers publish service intervals because oils, belts and fluids degrade on a schedule. Forget them, and the cost curve goes vertical.

Service on schedule. Always cheaper.
