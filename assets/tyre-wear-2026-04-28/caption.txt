Your tyres are talking. Most drivers never listen.

A tyre wears differently depending on what's wrong with the car. The pattern on the rubber tells you the cause. Once you know the four shapes, you can spot trouble before it spreads to your alignment, suspension, or fuel bill.

🔴 Center strip wear. The middle is shaved flat, shoulders look fine. Over-inflation. The tyre is bowing outward, only the centre touches road.

🔴 Both edges wear. Shoulders bald, centre still has tread. Under-inflation. The tyre is sagging, the edges take all the load.

🔴 One side only. Inner or outer edge is shaved off, the other side fine. Alignment. Toe or camber is off. Cheap to correct, expensive to ignore.

🔴 Patchy cupping. Random scalloped patches across the tread. Suspension. A tired shock or strut is letting the tyre bounce instead of grip.
