BMW 3-Series F30 · 2012-2019. The driver's sedan.

The F30 is the SG enthusiast's quiet pick. Sharp steering, balanced chassis, just enough comfort. But it rewards drivers and punishes neglect. Four to watch:

🔷 N20 timing chain. The 2.0 turbo is famous for chain stretch by 80,000 km. Cold rattle on start = act now, not later. Catastrophic if ignored.

🔷 Electric water pump. BMW's electric pump fails between 100,000-150,000 km, often without warning. Check before it strands you on the AYE.

🔷 VANOS solenoid. Rough idle, hesitation, check engine on? Often a clogged VANOS. Cleaning or replacement is cheaper than chasing other faults.

🔷 Oil filter housing gasket. The classic BMW leak. Oil drips down onto the alternator and belt. Replace at first sign of seepage.
